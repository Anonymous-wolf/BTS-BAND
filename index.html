THE BAND

Music for the soul

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that formed in Seoul in 2013. They have taken the world by storm with their powerful performances, touching lyrics, and energetic dance moves. BTS consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Known for their eclectic musical style and social message, they continue to captivate audiences worldwide.
